Welcome to the W&M AI for Skeptics Course

A self-paced learning experience designed for William & Mary faculty to navigate the evolving landscape of AI, explore its potential, and engage critically with its challenges.

Time Estimate: 70 minutes (course information); 190 minutes (if you complete application exercises)
Module 1: Fundamentals & Tools (20 min)
Module 2: Cognitive Concerns (15 min)
Module 3: Ethical Challenges (20 min)
Module 4: Summary & Guide (15 min)

Note: AI tools were used in the development of this course to assist with content structure and formatting.

Start the Course

Each module is entirely optional and can be completed in any order, though we recommend starting with Module 1 if you are new to AI. You are welcome to complete all four, revisit individual modules, or focus only on the topics most relevant to you.

A Note Before You Begin

The field of AI is moving quickly, and this course reflects our best understanding as of early 2026. Some tools, features, and statistics referenced throughout the modules may evolve over time. We encourage you to approach this material with curiosity and a critical eye.

We are glad you are here. Let's get started.
