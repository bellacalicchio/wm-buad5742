We Want to Hear From You

Thank you for taking the time to complete the AI Proficiency Course. Your feedback is one of the most valuable tools we have for making this course better — not just for you, but for every faculty member who takes it after you.

This course was built with the specific concerns and questions of William & Mary faculty in mind, and your honest perspective helps us ensure it stays that way. Whether you found a module particularly useful, felt something was missing, disagreed with how a topic was framed, or simply want to share what the experience was like, we want to know.

How Your Feedback Will Be Used

Every response is reviewed by our course development team. Your input directly informs how we update and improve the course content, which modules we expand or revise, and what new topics we might add in future versions. Feedback is collected anonymously unless you choose to include your name, and your responses will never be shared outside of the development team.

What We Are Especially Curious About

As you fill out the survey, here are some questions worth reflecting on:

  • Did the course address the concerns or skepticisms you came in with?
  • Was there a topic you wished had been covered in more depth?
  • Did your perspective on AI in education shift at all after completing the modules?
  • Would you recommend this course to a colleague?
